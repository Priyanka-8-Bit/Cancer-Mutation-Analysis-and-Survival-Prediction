How It Works
Pipeline Overview
01
Gene Matching — Selected gene mutations are matched against the 45 harmful genes identified in Stage 1 of the pipeline through gene-symbol overlap.
02
Mutation Score — The count of harmful genes mutated in this patient is computed as the Mutation Score. A higher score indicates more aggressive tumor biology.
03
Feature Engineering — An Age × Mutation Score interaction feature is computed. All features are standardized and PCA-transformed to 159 components.
04
Regression — Lasso Regression (best MAE: 483.6 days) predicts the estimated Days Till Death.
05
Classification — Stacking Ensemble (75.62% accuracy) predicts survival group: Short (<6 months) or Long (>18 months).
